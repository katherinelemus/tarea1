Chicken

Chicken breast is one of the most popular cuts of chicken. It’s high in protein and low in fat, making it an excellent choice for people trying to lose weight. One skinless, boneless, cooked chicken breast (172 grams) has the following nutrition breakdown (1):
Calories: 284
Protein: 53.4 grams
Carbs: 0 grams
Fat: 6.2 grams.

Beef

It is categorized as red meat — a term used for the meat of mammals, which contains higher amounts of iron than chicken or fish.
Usually eaten as roasts, ribs, or steaks, beef is also commonly ground or minced. Patties of ground beef are often used in hamburgers.
